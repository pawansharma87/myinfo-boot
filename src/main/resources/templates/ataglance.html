City At a Glance

District Dholpur is situated in the eastern part of Rajasthan. The present name is said to have been derived from Dhaulpur as a separate district came into existence in 1982 comprising four tehsils of Bharatpur namely Dholpur, Rajakhera, Bari and Baseri. It is bordered by Bharatpur district of Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh to the north, Madhya Pradesh to the south, Karauli district to the west and Uttar Pradesh and Madhya Pradesh to the east. The District has five Subdivisions and four development Blocks namely Dholpur, Bari, Baseri and Rajakhera. The district is well connected by roads(NH-3 Agra-Mumbai) and railways. The Broad-gauge lines of Mumbai ? Agra of North Central Railways, passes through the district headquarter Dholpur.
