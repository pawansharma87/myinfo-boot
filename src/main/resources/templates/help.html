Frequently Asked Questions

If you are facing any problem using this website, you might find your answers here or if still you cant find the answer, we are always up there for your help. You can write to us in the - Contacts Page

Q: How can i create my own account here?

A: To create your account, you can follow these simple steps:

- Go to Home Page.
- You'll see a black coloured box on the left side.



Q: How to Login?

A: To login into your account, you can follow these simple steps:

- Go to Home Page.
- You'll see a black coloured box on the left side.
- If you are a new user here, you can simply create your account (refer to the above question).
- If you have already created an account, click on 'Sign In' tab, Enter your user name and password and click 'Submit' button.
